                   ALPU LICENSE CHANGELOG
                    © 2025 Asuka Org

All notable changes to the ALPU License project will be
documented in this file. This includes updates to the main
license (ALPU), as well as its variants (ALEPU, ALLPU).

[ALPU v1.2] — 2025-11-23
---

- Updated COPYRIGHT and GOVERNING LAW fields for clarity:
    * Copyright: © 2025 Asuka Org
    * Governing Law: California, United States
- Clarified derivative work rules to prevent closed-source
  relicensing without documented permission.
- Added formal enforcement clause for sublicensing.
- Minor formatting and editorial improvements in LICENSE_ALPU.txt.
- Expanded SPEC.md and PROJECT_NOTICE.md to reflect v1.2 rules.
- GOVERNANCE.md and CONTRIBUTING.md now formalized for version control.

[ALPU v1.1] — 2025-11-23
---

- Added explicit modification and redistribution restrictions.
- Defined derivative works permissions and licensing requirements.
- Introduced Patent Grant section (optional).
- Initial draft of LICENSE_ALPU.txt, SPEC.md, and PROJECT_NOTICE.md.

[ALPU v1.0] — 2025-11-23

- Initial release of ALPU License.
- Base structure:
    * Permission granted for personal and commercial use
    * Redistribution allowed only in original, unmodified form
    * Modification requires explicit permission
    * Disclaimer of liability included
- Versioning framework established for future updates.

[ALEPU v1.0] — 2025-11-23
---
- First release of Asuka License for Extensive Protective Use.
- Additional restrictions beyond ALPU to increase protection.
- Compatibility checked against ALPU v1.2.

[ALLPU v1.0] — 2025-11-23
---

- First release of Asuka License for Loosely Protective Use.
- Allows modifications as long as modified code remains open-source.
- Redistribution allowed; closed-source requires explicit permission.
